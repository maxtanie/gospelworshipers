Story

About
Gospel singer famous as the oldest member of his family's Grammy-nominated quartet The Walls Group. He made major contributions to his group's debut studio album, Fast Forward.
Before Fame
He formed The Walls Group when he was in his teens and released the group's self-titled first recording at the age of 18.
Trivia
Shortly after its September 2014 release, The Walls Group's album Fast Forward rose to number thirty-nine on the U.S. Billboard 200 and to number one on the U.S. Gospel Chart.
Family Life
He and his siblings Rhea, Alic, and Ahjah Walls formed The Walls Group in their hometown of Houston, Texas.
Associated With
He and Erica Atkins-Campbell are both famous as members of sibling gospel groups.
